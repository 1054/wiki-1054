If flux F is in unit Jy km/s, then 

$ \frac{L}{[erg\ s^{-1}]} = \frac{F}{[Jy\ km/s]} \cdot \frac{[km/s]}{c} \cdot \frac{\nu_{obs}}{[Hz]} \cdot 4 \pi \frac{D_L^2}{[cm]^2} $

$ \frac{L}{[erg\ s^{-1}]} = 3.9910769 \times 10^{30} \cdot \frac{F}{[Jy\ km/s]} \cdot \frac{\nu_{obs}}{[GHz]} \cdot \frac{D_L^2}{[Mpc]^2} $

$ \frac{L}{[L_{sun}]} = 1.0339577 \times 10^{-3} \cdot \frac{F}{[Jy\ km/s]} \cdot \frac{\nu_{obs}}{[GHz]} \cdot \frac{D_L^2}{[Mpc]^2} $

note that $\nu_{obs}$ is the observed frequency, rather than the rest frame frequency.
