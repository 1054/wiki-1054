$ L_{MoleLine} = 4 \pi D_L^2 F_{obs} = 4 \pi D_L^2 \ \nu_{obs} \ c^{-1} ( \Sigma \Delta S_{obs} v_{obs} ) \quad [L_{\odot}] $

$ L_{MoleLine}^{'} = Area * F_{source} = \Omega_{s} D_A^2 (\Sigma T_{s} \Delta v_{s}) = D_L^2 \ \nu_{obs}^{-2} \ \frac{c^2}{2k_B} (1+z)^{-3} ( \Sigma \Delta S_{obs} v_{obs} ) \quad [K \ km/s \ pc^2] $

where $ ( \Sigma \Delta S_{obs} v_{obs} ) $ is in unit of $ [Jy km/s] $. 

$ L_{MoleLine}^{'} = Area * F_{source} = \Omega_{s} D_A^2 (\Sigma T_{s} \Delta v_{s}) = \Omega_{s*b} D_A^2 \ I_{obs} \ (1+z) \quad [K \ km/s \ pc^2] $

where $ I_{obs} $ is in unit of $ [K km/s] $.
